Log Files

VigoSurge adheres to a conventional method that involves the utilization of log files. These files record the activities of visitors who navigate websites. This is a common practice among hosting companies and forms a key part of the analytics provided by hosting services. Information gathered through log files consists of internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser types, Internet Service Provider (ISP), timestamps, referring/exit pages, and potentially the number of clicks. Importantly, this data is not connected to any personally identifiable information. The primary objectives of collecting this information include trend analysis, site administration, user behavior tracking, and obtaining demographic insights.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Just like all other websites, VigoSurge utilizes 'cookies'. These cookies are employed to retain details such as visitors' preferences, and which pages on the platform the visitor viewed or accessed. This data is utilized to enhance users' browsing experience by tailoring our webpage content according to visitors' browser type and other relevant information.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Our website collaborates with Google as a third-party vendor. Google employs cookies, specifically DART cookies, to display targeted advertisements to visitors of our site depending on their interactions with www.website.com and other online platforms. Visitors have the option to opt out of DART cookie usage by accessing Google's ad and content network Privacy Policy at the given web address: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Children’s Information

Ensuring the safety of children online is another area we focus on. We advise parents and guardians to actively engage in overseeing, participating in, and supervising their children’s internet usage.

VigoSurge is committed to not gathering any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13 knowingly.
